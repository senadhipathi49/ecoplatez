Who We Are

Ecoplatez is an India-based startup committed to reducing single-use plastic through innovative, sustainable tableware. By working directly with farming communities, we create beautiful, biodegradable plates that support both the planet and local livelihoods.


Our mission is to provide an accessible and attractive alternative to disposable plastic while creating sustainable income opportunities for rural communities.
